Sweet Potato Casserole

Description

Sweet potatoes topped with creamy toasted marshmallows.

The calories per serving: 267 calories; protein 2g; carbohydrates 60.4g; fat 2.6g; sodium 143mg

Ingredients

  • 5 sweet potatoes, sliced
  • 1/4 cup reduced fat margarine
  • 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
  • 3 tablespoon orange juice
  • 1 pich ground cinnamon
  • 1 (10.5 ounce) package miniature marshmallows

Steps

  1. Step1

    2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

  2. Step2

    3. Place sweet potatoes in a large saucepan with enough water to cover. Bring to a boil, and cook until tender, about 15 minutes. Remove from heat, drain, and mash.

  3. Step3

    4. Place mashed sweet potatoes in large bowl, and use an electric mixer to blend with the margarine, brown sugar, orange juice, and cinnamon. Spread evenly into a 9x13 inch baking dish. Top with miniature marshmallows.

  4. Step4

    5. Bake for 25 to 30 minutes in the preheated oven, or until heated through, and marshmallows are puffed and golden brown.

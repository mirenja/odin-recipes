Meatloaf

Description

This is a fairly easy and no fail recipe for meatloaf.it doesnt take long to make and serves about 8 people

The calories per serving: 372 calories; protein 18.2g; carbohydrates 18.5g; fat 24.7g; cholesterol 98mg; sodium 334.6mg. Full Nutrition

Ingredients

  • 1.31 Pounds ground beef
  • .88 egg
  • .88 onion , chopped
  • .75 cup and 2 tablespoons milk
  • .75 cup and 2 tablespoons dried bread crumbs
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1.5 tablespoons and 0.75 teaspoon brown sugar
  • 1.5 tablespoons and 0.75 teaspoon prepared mustard
  • .25 cup and 1 tablespoon ketchup

Steps

  1. Step1

    2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

  2. Step2

    3. In a large bowl, combine the beef, egg, onion, milk and bread OR cracker crumbs. Season with salt and pepper to taste and place in a lightly greased 9x5-inch loaf pan, or form into a loaf and place in a lightly greased 9x13-inch baking dish.

  3. Step3

    4. In a separate small bowl, combine the brown sugar, mustard and ketchup. Mix well and pour over the meatloaf.

  4. Step4

    5. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 1 hour.
